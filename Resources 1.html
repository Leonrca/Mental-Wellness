Helpful External Resources

National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)

NAMI provides advocacy, education, support and public awareness so that all individuals and families affected by mental illness can build better lives.

MentalHealth.gov

U.S. government website providing one-stop access to U.S. government mental health and mental health problems information.

Self-Care Strategies

Mindfulness Meditation

Practice being present in the moment without judgment. Start with just 5 minutes a day.

Physical Activity

Regular exercise can help reduce anxiety and depression. Find an activity you enjoy.

Healthy Sleep Habits

Prioritize 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night for optimal mental health.
